Windows only · Requires MTG Arena

Server Browser

Find games in formats Arena doesn't support

Browse and join public lobbies for Pauper, Modern, Planar Standard, and more. Create a lobby, share a link, or list it publicly. Other MTGA+ users can join with one click. No friend requests needed.

Format Enforcement

Deck checks powered by Scryfall

When you select a deck in a format lobby, MTGA+ validates every card against the format's legal card list. Illegal cards are flagged instantly. No more honor-system format enforcement.

Quality of Life

Reduce visual clutter

Disable flashy card entrance animations, 3D model popups, and pet sounds. Keep the core gameplay readable without the visual noise. Toggle on or off anytime from the MTGA+ settings panel.

Cross-Platform

Play with anyone, even without the app

Players on Mac, mobile, or without MTGA+ can still browse lobbies from the web and request an invite by entering their username. The host's client sends them a standard in-game challenge invite automatically.
